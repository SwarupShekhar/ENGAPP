What’s new (2026-07-08)

  • Honest post-call wait: after ending a call you’ll see a checklist (Speech quality → Grammar → Vocabulary → Pronunciation → Final report) instead of a fake progress %. Items light up when the server actually finishes each piece.
  • Partner still on the call? You’ll get a clear “Waiting for your partner” screen — not fake AI progress.
  • Pronunciation can keep refining for a short time after scores appear — that’s normal (async audio analysis).

Why we changed this: the old % climbed while nothing finished, so people thought the app froze. Full notes for testers: RELEASE-NOTES-2026-07-08-feedback-loading.md on the deploy.

2. What to try

  • Sign in → Home → daily phrase/word listen
  • Maya tutor — speak naturally for 3–5 minutes
  • Optional: one P2P call with another tester
  • After any call: watch the feedback loading checklist (and try leaving partner on the call once to see partner-wait)

Known rough edges: Maya can be slow on first turn; full analysis may still take up to ~2 minutes. You should not see a Progress % on feedback loading anymore — if you do, report it as a bug.
